Campus Ambassador Program

Lead the hardware revolution on your campus

About the Program

CORETEXELA Campus Ambassadors are student leaders who bring quality VLSI, Embedded, and IoT education to their colleges. You'll host workshops, build communities, and help peers gain industry-ready skills—while developing your own leadership experience.

Program Benefits

Leadership Experience

Lead tech workshops, organize events, and build a community of 20+ students on your campus. Perfect for your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Exclusive Resources

Get early access to premium content, industry webinars, and mentorship from hardware professionals before public release.

Network with Industry

Direct connections with companies like Infineon, TCS, and Qualcomm. Priority access to job opportunities and internships.

Recognition & Certification

Official CORETEXELA Ambassador certificate, featured on our website, and letter of recommendation for outstanding performers.

Responsibilities

  • Host 2-4 workshops or study sessions per month on VLSI/Embedded/IoT topics
  • Recruit and onboard 20+ students from your college into CORETEXELA community
  • Share learning resources, project ideas, and job opportunities with your campus network
  • Provide monthly feedback on curriculum, platform features, and student needs
  • Represent CORETEXELA at college tech fests and events (optional)

Who Should Apply?

We're looking for passionate ECE/EEE students (2nd-4th year) who love hardware, enjoy teaching, and want to make a real impact on their campus. No prior experience needed—just enthusiasm and commitment!
