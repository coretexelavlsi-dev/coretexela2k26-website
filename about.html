About CORETEXELA

Building India's premier VLSI, Embedded Systems, and IoT learning platform

Our Mission

We're building India's premier VLSI/Embedded/IoT learning platform to bridge the 70% skills gap in hardware engineering. Through community-driven, free-first education, we're empowering the next generation of India's semiconductor and IoT workforce.

Meet the Founders

Teja Reddy

Co-Founder, Full-Stack Hardware Engineer

Teja is passionate about democratizing hardware education in India. With deep expertise in VLSI design and embedded systems, he recognized that most engineering students graduate without industry-ready skills. CORETEXELA is his mission to change that—making cutting-edge hardware knowledge accessible to every student, regardless of their college or budget.

Pooja

Co-Founder, Community Lead

Pooja leads CORETEXELA's community strategy, building the campus ambassador program and student engagement initiatives. She believes peer-to-peer learning is the key to scaling impact across India's 4,000+ engineering colleges. Her vision: turn every engaged student into a community leader who brings quality hardware education to their campus.

Our Approach

Community-First

We build with students, not for them. Our 248+ member community guides every decision. From curriculum design to feature priorities, student feedback shapes CORETEXELA's evolution.

Free-First

Core learning materials remain free forever. Premium certifications and advanced modules come later, but only after students experience real value. We believe education is a right, not a luxury.

India-Focused

We partner with Indian semiconductor companies and align our curriculum with local industry needs. From Infineon to TCS, our job board connects students with real opportunities in India's growing hardware sector.

Job-Integrated

Learning paths lead directly to job opportunities. Students don't just learn theory—they build portfolios, connect with recruiters, and transition from campus to industry with confidence.
